S'mores Trail Mix

The Perfect Campfire Reminiscent Snack

Whether you just want a yummy snack to eat while watching TV, or a snack for the campfire, this recipe is for you!

Instead of using the ingredients in a standard trail mix such as nuts, seeds, and chocolate, this recipe uses the same ingredients used to make traditional s'mores. With just 5 minutes of preparation, enjoy the taste of s'mores without the campfire.

Ingredients

  • 2 c grahm crackers
  • 1 c mini marshmallows
  • 1/2 c chocolate chips
  • 1 c Chocolate Toast Crunch

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a large mixing bowl.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Store the s'mores trail mix in reusable containers.
